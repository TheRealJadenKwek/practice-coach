# Practice Coach v1.0.0

First public release — a data-driven Practice-Mode coach for Geometry Dash.

It records every attempt, auto-clusters where you die into hotspot sections, and tells you what to work on next — adapting to how you're actually playing each level.

## Highlights
- 🎯 **Adaptive goal-setting** — follows your *growing edge* on levels you chain forward, switches to *drill your worst wall* on harder projects you grind backwards, and flips to *go for the clear* when you reliably reach the end.
- 🔥 **Auto hotspots + heat strip** — deaths cluster into sections with no checkpoints to place; a green→red consistency strip with a live cursor shows the whole level at a glance.
- 🧠 **Skill-aware Coach screen** — estimates your skill from the AREDL demon ladder, shows what you're closest to clearing, and recommends what to play next (tap to jump in).
- 💾 Per-attempt save-to-disk and correct percent tracking — your history survives crashes, and nothing leaves your machine.

## Requirements
- Geometry Dash **2.2081**, Geode **5.7.x**, **Windows**.

## Install
Download `jaden.practice-coach.geode` below and drop it onto the in-game Geode mods list (or place it in `Geometry Dash/geode/mods/`), then restart the game. Classic (percent) levels only.
